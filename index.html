About IZ*ONE

IZ*ONE (pronounced "eyes one") was a highly successful South Korean-Japanese
project girl group formed through the reality competition show Produce 48.

The group consisted of 12 members and was active from October 29, 2018, until
their planned disbandment on April 29, 2021.
